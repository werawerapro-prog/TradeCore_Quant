## 📖 What is TradeCore_Quant?

TradeCore_Quant is a software application designed to help you understand and use trading techniques without needing to code. It works on Windows computers and makes it easier to watch market trends, test trading ideas, and manage financial risks.

It uses straightforward math tools to analyze stock and currency data. You can run it on your PC to see how different trading methods might work.

This guide walks you through downloading and starting the app step-by-step.

## 🔍 Using Technical Indicators

Indicators help you understand market movement:

- **SMA (Simple Moving Average):** Shows average price over time.
- **EMA (Exponential Moving Average):** Reacts faster to price changes.
- **RSI (Relative Strength Index):** Measures speed and change of price movements.
- **MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence):** Highlights trend shifts.

You can turn these indicators on or off in the app and adjust settings to fit your trading style.
